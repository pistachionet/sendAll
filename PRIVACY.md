# Privacy Policy for sendAll

**Effective Date:** December 27, 2025

**sendAll** ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.**

- **Contacts**: The app accesses your contacts **locally on your device** solely to allow you to select recipients for your messages. This data is never sent to any external server.
- **Messages**: Your messages are composed and sent directly via your device's native messaging capabilities (SMS/iMessage). We do not see, store, or have access to the content of your messages.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The app operates entirely on-device and does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

## 3. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
